Start day tracking to categorize your entire day into focused work, downtime, and idle time.

## When to use

Use at the start of your work day to:

- Track total focused time vs downtime vs idle time
- Get an end-of-day summary of how you spent your time
- See patterns in your work habits

## Auto-start

Day tracking auto-starts if you begin your first focus session before 10am.

## What it tracks

- **Focused time**: Time spent in active focus sessions
- **Downtime**: Time in downtime sessions (breaks, maintenance)
- **Idle time**: Gaps between sessions (auto-calculated)

## Examples

- `/focus-day-start`

## CLI equivalent

```bash
nilai day-start
```
