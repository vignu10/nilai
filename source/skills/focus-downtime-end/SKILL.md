End the current downtime session and display a summary.

## When to use

Use when you're done with your break/downtime and ready to:
- Start a new focus session
- End your work day
- Take a different type of break

## What it shows

- Downtime type (break, maintenance, awaiting)
- Duration of downtime
- Whether you stayed within your max duration limit

## Examples

- `/focus-downtime-end`

## CLI equivalent

```bash
nilai downtime-end
```
